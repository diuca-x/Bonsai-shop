Bonsai

$19.99

The purposes of bonsai are primarily contemplation for
the viewer, and the pleasant exercise of effort and ingenuity for
the grower.

By constrast with other plant cultivation practices, bonsai is
not intended for production of food or for medicine. Instead,
bonsai practice focuses on long-term cultivation shaping
of one or more small trees growing in a container.
